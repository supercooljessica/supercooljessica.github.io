About Me

Hello! My name is Jessica and I am a recent college graduate with a love for math, sports, and making things. Currently, I work as a full-time teaching assistant at Harvard University for multiple courses in mathematics and digital fabrication. This website serves as documentation of my many ongoing projects, from a squirrel photo booth to a CNC-routed wooden pillow! I am particularly interested in showcasing the beauty of mathematics and its applications to all aspects of human life.
