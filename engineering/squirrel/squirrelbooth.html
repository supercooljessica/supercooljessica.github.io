Squirrel Photo Booth

Completed: June 15, 2020

List of materials:

  • Arduino Leonardo
  • Breadboard
  • iPhone with headphone jack
  • Phone tripod
  • Selfie stick with button
  • 3D printer limit switch
  • Cardboard scraps
  • Honey Bunches of Oats

Description:

This device is a pressure-activated camera system. It relies on the fact that the iPhone registers a short in the audio jack as a volume change, which in the camera app, activates the shutter. A cardboard platform was glued onto a scrap 3D printer limit switch, which then closed when activated by a small weight.
