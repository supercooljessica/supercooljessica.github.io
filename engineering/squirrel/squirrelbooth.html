Squirrel Photo Booth

Completed: June 2020

List of materials:

  • Arduino Leonardo
  • Breadboard
  • iPhone with headphone jack
  • Phone tripod
  • Selfie stick with button
  • Limit switch
  • Cardboard scraps
  • Honey Bunches of Oats

Description:

Squirrels are fascinating creatures! Inspired by Mark Rober's squirrel obstacle courses, I wanted to make a similar device to interact with my cute, furry neighbors. This device is a pressure-activated camera system which snaps a photo when a weight is detected on the base. The cardboard base lies on top of a limit switch, so a small weight closes the circuit and is read by the microcontroller. An old iPhone is hooked up via the audio jack to take a photo when the sensor is activated. This project was completed during COVID, so there were lots of squirrels out and about, and I ended up getting some great shots!
